representin is a song by american hip hop recording artist ludacris featuring vocals from american singer-songwriter kelly rowland it was released october 9 2012 in promotion of his ninth studio album ludaversal the song debuted and peaked at number 97 on the billboard hot 100 while reaching number 28 on the hot r&b/hip-hop songs the official remix features the r&b singer r kelly and the american rapper fabolous
