mick donnellan (born c 1970) is an irish playwright noir writer and theatre director
a native of ballinrobe county mayo donnellan is a graduate of national university of ireland galway's ma in creative writing he has written four performed plays and a noir crime novel set in galway (he cites ken bruen as an influence)
