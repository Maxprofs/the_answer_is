reftinskaya gres is a coal-fired power plant (gres is a russia-specific term) located near the reftinsky settlement in sverdlovsk oblast russia with its installed capacity of 3,800 mw reftinskaya gres is considered russias most powerful thermal power station the station is of a condenser type and uses an adjacent 25 square kilometer pond to feed its condensers the stations chimney 4 is ranked among the highest in the world with its height of some 320 meters
the station belongs to enel russia a russian power generation company majority-owned by enel of italy
construction on the plant began in 1963 with the first power generating unit launched in 1970
