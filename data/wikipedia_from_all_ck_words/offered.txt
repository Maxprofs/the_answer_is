in the mathematical theory of probability offered load is a concept in queuing theory the offered load is a measure of traffic in the queue the offered load is given by little's law the arrival rate into the queue (symbolized with ) multiplied by the mean holding time (symbolized with ) the average amount of time spent by items in the queue offered load is expressed in erlang units or call-seconds per hour a dimensionless measure
