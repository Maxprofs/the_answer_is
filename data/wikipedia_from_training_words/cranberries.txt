tonight belongs to u! is a song by american singer jeremih it features american rapper flo rida and was released as a promotional single prior to his third studio album late nights the track premiered on april 6 2015
== remixesedit ==
on june 4 2015 dutch dj afrojack released a remix of the track
== performancesedit ==
jeremih performed the song with flo rida on the tonight show starring jimmy fallon july 22 2015
