carlos atanes (born november 8 1971 in barcelona spain) is a spanish film director writer and playwright his first finished feature-length movie is faq frequently asked questions which he released in 2004 the movie won the best feature film award at the athens panorama of independent filmmakers in 2005 and was also nominated for the mlis d'argent at fantasporto that same year
