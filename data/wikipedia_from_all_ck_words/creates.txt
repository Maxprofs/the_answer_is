creative recycling enviro-arts training and education service
in partnership with warwickshire county council artists in warwickshire education and trees are green (tag) creates is warwickshires first creative recycling centre for schools pre-schools further education and community groups as a new initiative linking arts and education the resource has been at its home at tag wedgnock industrial estate warwick for over 12 months
creates encourages an innovative and pro-active approach to creativity and environmentalism exploring reject materials faulty products and otherwise worthless objects to develop new opportunities for communication and creativity in the classroom and beyond it is a collaboration between the world of industry commerce public authorities educational and community services and the world of art culture and environmental agencies creates is a cultural project - and a place for promoting the idea that waste materials can be used as resources
