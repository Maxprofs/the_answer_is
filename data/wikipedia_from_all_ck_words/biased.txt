biled is a commune in timi county romania it is composed of a single village biled it also included two other villages until 2004 when they were split off to form andra commune
== name ==
in german it is known as billed in hungarian as billd and in serbian as / biled
== ethnic groups ==
in 2002 the population was composed of
romanians (90.75%)
roma people (3.04%)
hungarians (2.89%)
germans (2.76%)
== religion ==
in 2002 the population was composed of
orthodox christians (79.89%)
roman catholics (9.56%)
