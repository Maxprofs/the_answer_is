watched is a horror video game developed by gaming online now
the game is part horror and simulator to show how the logic of the brain reacts to darkness and horror it was released in january 2014 for linux microsoft windows and mac os x
== trailer ==
the official trailer to the game was released on 6 december 2013 including some pieces from the game itself the trailer is able be to watched on youtube on the channel gaming online now
== gameplay ==
in watched players must try to get through the levels with little clues found around the map of levels.there are different levels around the game including woods,maze and boss level
