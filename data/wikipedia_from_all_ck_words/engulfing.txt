widow * second wife real sucking engulfing a rare utensil ( goke * gozai seishaburi meikimeguri) is a 2004 japanese pink film directed by shigeo moriyama and scripted by kazuhiro sano who also plays a lead role in the film it won the award for eighth best film at the pink grand prix ceremony
== synopsis ==
a lecherous priest lives in the countryside with his beautiful wife yoshiko jealous of her husband's unfaithfulness yoshiko plots murderous revenge

== availability ==
shigeo moriyama filmed widow * second wife real sucking engulfing a rare utensil for yutaka ikejima's cement match production studio and it was released theatrically in japan by op eiga on march 30 2004 it became available through the hokuto corporation's online service dmm on april 15 2005 op gave the film a second theatrical release on august 17 2007 under the title shikij bzu no goke kuzushi ()
