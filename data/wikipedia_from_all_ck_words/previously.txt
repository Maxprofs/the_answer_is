the bombesin receptors are a group of g-protein coupled receptors which bind bombesin
three bombesin receptors are currently known:bb1 previously known as neuromedin b receptor nmbrbb2 previously known as gastrin-releasing peptide receptor grprbb3 previously known as bombesin-like receptor 3 brs3
