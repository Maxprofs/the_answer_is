eventually is the second solo album that paul westerberg released after the breakup of the replacements
the album started out in atlanta with producer brendan o'brien although those sessions produced good resultsthe leadoff single love untold among themwesterberg and o'brien parted ways o'brien was pressed for time and westerberg needed more time to write enough songs to fill out a full album westerberg regrouped with lou giordano
the song good day was written for late replacements guitarist bob stinson it references hold my life a track from the album tim
