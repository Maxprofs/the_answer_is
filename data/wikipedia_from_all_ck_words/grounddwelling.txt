lacinipolia sareta is a moth in the noctuidae family it is found throughout western north america from the southern yukon and the northwest territories to texas arizona and california it probably also occurs in northern mexico it ranges eastward across the southern boreal region to at least quebec
adults are similar to lacinipolia dimocki but are smaller and have duller white hindwings adults have been recorded on wing from june to july and again from late september to october in arizona new mexico and texas
the larvae are grounddwelling and polyphagous
