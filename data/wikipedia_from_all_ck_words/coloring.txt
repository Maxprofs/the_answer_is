you're nearer is an american popular song by composer richard rodgers and lyricist lorenz hart (rodgers and hart) from the 1940 film version of the broadway musical too many girls
== covers ==
june christy - ballads for night people/the intimate miss christy (1998)
versions of the song appear on the album judy at carnegie hall and subsequently the live tribute album rufus does judy at carnegie hall
jeri southern - southern hospitality/jeri gently jumps (2008)
