july (/dla/ juu-ly) is the seventh month of the year (between june and august) in the julian and gregorian calendars and one of seven months with the length of 31 days it was named by the roman senate in honor of the roman general julius caesar it being the month of his birth prior to that it was called quintilis
it is on average the warmest month in most of the northern hemisphere (where it is the second month of summer) and the coldest month in much of the southern hemisphere (where it is the second month of winter) the second half of the year commences in july in the southern hemisphere july is the seasonal equivalent of january in the northern hemisphere
in the northern hemisphere dog days are considered to begin in early july when the hot sultry weather of summer usually starts spring lambs born in late winter or early spring are usually sold before july 1

== july symbols ==
july's birthstone is the ruby which symbolizes contentment
its birth flowers are the larkspur or the water lily
the zodiac signs for the month of july are cancer (until july 22) and leo (july 23 onwards)
