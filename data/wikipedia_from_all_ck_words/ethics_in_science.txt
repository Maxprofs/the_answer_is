the avicenna prize for ethics in science is awarded every two years by unesco and rewards individuals and groups in the field of ethics in science
the aim of the award is to promote ethical reflection on issues raised by advances in science and technology and to raise global awareness of the importance of ethics in science the prize was named after the 11th century persian physician and philosopher abu ali al-husain ibn abdallah ibn sina (980-1038) otherwise known as avicenna
the prize consists of a gold medal a certificate usd$10,000 and a one-week academic visit to the islamic republic of iran
since it was founded the award has been given to three individuals
== award winners ==
2004 margaret somerville canada
2006 abdallah s daar canada
2010 renzong qiu china
2015 dr zaabta khan shinwari pakistan
